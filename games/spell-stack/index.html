How to play Spell Stack

A 3×3 grid of runes lights up in a sequence. Each rune has a position and a color. Reproduce the sequence by tapping cells, but follow the directive shown above the grid.

Red Yellow Green Blue
  • FORWARD — tap cells in the same order they lit up.
  • REVERSE — tap cells in reverse order. (1.5× score)
  • ALPHA — tap by color in alphabetical order: Blue → Green → Red → Yellow. Same color, earlier first. (2× score)

Each correct tap scores. A perfect round bumps the spell length up by one and adds a bonus. A mistake drops the length by one (minimum 3). Eight rounds, five-minute cap.
